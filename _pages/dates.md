Use U.S. format: month, day, year. Abbreviate the month when it precedes a date:

<em>Dec. 21</em>

<em>Mar. 17, 2025</em>

<em>Thursday, Mar. 17</em>

In general, use relative rather than specific dates: <em>on Sunday, last Wednesday</em>
